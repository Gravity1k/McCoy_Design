Maintenance

With our subscription, you get access to on-call maintenance for any issues that may arise. Just send us a text or email and we will respond and fix the problem as soon as we can. Our subscription also includes up to 8 updates a month. This encompasses any new posts or content you would like to add, such as articles, imagery, or sections. We also offer revisions of your site at an hourly rate. Revisions are things like complete layout changes, new branding, or rebuilding of the site into an updated version. Every 3 years we offer free revisions to your site, so you’ll never be out of date. For non-subscribing customers, we offer scheduled maintenance for free, and all other services are at an hourly rate.
