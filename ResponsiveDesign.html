Responsive Design

In the growing age of technology, mobile phones rank #1 in internet surfing. About 92% of internet browsing is done on mobile devices, so having a website that is responsive is essential. Responsiveness refers to the sites ability to adapt to the screen size of the device its being displayed on. When making a site responsive, we also take into account the page speed for mobile devices vs desktop. While a website may run great on your computer, if it isn’t adapted to run on phones and tablets, customers trying to reach your business on those devices will be turned away for a multitude of issues they might encounter. We make your site load instantly, look, run and feel good on all devices.
