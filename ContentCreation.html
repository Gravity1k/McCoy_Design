Content for Your Site

Along with building you a website, we also offer extra services. These services are billed at an hourly rate, and include logo design, branding, creating copy, content research and gathering. Your website will need imagery and copy. We are more than happy to provide these for you if you are missing a few pieces or just want us to take care of it all! Otherwise you are welcome to provide all of the content for your website. Along with this, we offer branding and logo design, at our discretion depending on what you are looking for. This includes choosing your color scheme, creating a vector image logo, creating alternative logos and themes for the website.
